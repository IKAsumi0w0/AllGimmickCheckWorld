【How to Use】
Please drag and drop the Prefab into your scene.

Materials are already assigned.

Textures are provided at a high resolution of 2048×2048.

You may freely adjust material settings to better match your scene.
This asset uses the Standard Shader, and the MetallicSmoothness texture is used in the metallic (R) and smoothness (A) channels.
Feel free to tweak the shader properties to suit your lighting and rendering style.
